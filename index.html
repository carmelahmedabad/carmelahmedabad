ABOUT US

CARMEL educational group was founded in 1994, with a vision to provide the best quality education to secondary and higher secondary students of Mumbai suburbs. 23 years later CARMEL remains one of the best institutions among the organised classroom coaching centres in Mumbai suburbs.

CARMEL provides premium personalised tuitions for the Std. IXth, Xth students of GSEB and CBSE. Finding personal for the child is extremely important and requires careful judgement based on several parameters.

At CARMEL learning takes place in small groups or one to one. Students are able to focus better and are taught in caring environment that meets their own unique requirements.

CARMEL’s faculty comprises of highly qualified, talented and experienced professors. Faculty is mentored to achieve and maintain the standards of content delivery and teaching.

All this, along with result-oriented teaching methodologies, comprehensive evaluation systems and a high rate of academic success, distinguishes CARMEL as one of the leaders in education.
